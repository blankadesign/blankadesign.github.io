Tropical Fruit Juice

We did the branding for this pioneer of healthy beverages.


The Result

Tropical Fruit Juice is a franchise of juice bars with stores in several shopping malls. It offers a variety of fruit juices, smoothies and yogurts.

The goals was to bring the experiences of drinking freshly made juices from the country side to the city. We chose to emphasize their core values: freshness, natural and fun.

A fresh, natural and colorful design.
